Something not working? Start here.

## Network issues

If you can't get online or something feels off, check the [Network Troubleshooting guide](/network-troubleshooting/). It covers everything from cable checks to talking to staff.

Quick version:
1. Cable in? Link light on?
2. Can you open [spawn.ctrl-alt-gg.hu](https://spawn.ctrl-alt-gg.hu)?
3. Can you reach [servers.ctrl-alt-gg.hu](https://servers.ctrl-alt-gg.hu)?

If yes to all three, your connection is fine and the problem is elsewhere. If not, follow the guide or tell staff.

Do **not** change network settings, swap cables between ports, or plug in your own switches or routers.

## Game problems

- **Game won't launch**: Verify files, update drivers, check firewall prompts.
- **Can't connect to a server**: Check [servers.ctrl-alt-gg.hu](https://servers.ctrl-alt-gg.hu) first. If that server shows as up, the issue is likely on your end.
- **Terrible performance**: Close background apps, check for updates running, and make sure your power plan is set to high performance.
- **Want to play something not on the list?** Go for it. If you and some friends want to set something up, nobody will stop you. If you need a local server, ask staff and we'll see what we can do.

## Hardware and desk stuff

- You get up to **two power outlets**. If you need more devices powered, bring a power strip.
- Do **not** bring a UPS or plug in speakers.
- Label your cables and gear. You're responsible for your own equipment — don't touch other people's machines.
- Don't block walkways with your setup. Ask staff if you need help routing cables.
- If something breaks or sparks, unplug it and tell staff immediately.

## Food and drink

Everyone handles their own meals. If people want to order together, that usually gets organized at the venue. Cold drinks and snacks are available at cost.

Eat and drink **only in designated areas** — not at your rig.

## Other help

- **Need to rest?** Find a quiet spot. Life goes on around you, but nobody will judge you for taking a break.
- **Transport trouble?** Bring it up on Discord. Community usually sorts these things out.
- **Something bothering you?** Talk to an organizer directly. We handle things quickly and privately.

## When to just tell staff

- No network at all
- Electrical problems
- Someone being a jerk
- Anything that feels unsafe or seriously wrong

Don't overthink it. If you're unsure, just ask.
