Left your mouse at home? Keyboard died? There is a solution.

## Borrowing

1. **Ask around:** The LAN community is helpful. Your neighbor might have a spare mouse in their bag.
2. **Organizer Desk:** We have a "Lost & Found" box that sometimes contains unclaimed (or donated) cables and peripherals. Ask us.
3. **Discord:** Post in `#general` or `#support`: "Looking to borrow an HDMI cable for 2 hours."

## The Rules

- **Return it.** If you borrow something, bring it back immediately when you are done.
- **Take care of it.** Do not rage-smash a borrowed keyboard.
- **Say thanks.** An energy drink or chocolate bar is the standard exchange rate for a life-saving cable.

## Mark your gear

There will be 100 black cables and 50 identical mice on the tables.

- **Tape / Sticker:** Put a small piece of colored tape on the end of your cable and the bottom of your mouse.
- **Name:** Write your name with a marker (if you don't mind), or on the tape.
- **Photo:** Take a picture of your setup before you start packing on Sunday. This way you know what was yours.

If you find something that isn't yours, bring it to the Organizer Desk.
