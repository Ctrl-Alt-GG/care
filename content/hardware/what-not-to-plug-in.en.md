These will cause problems for everyone. Don't bring them.

## 1. Speakers

- **Use a headset.**
- Nobody wants to hear your game, music, or voice chat.
- This is non-negotiable.

## 2. UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply)

- They draw huge current when charging.
- They cause harmonic distortion on the power grid.
- If power goes out, it goes out for everyone. Your UPS won't save the LAN.

## 3. Network Gear

- **No routers.**
- **No switches.**
- **No access points.**
- Plugging these in can create IP conflicts, loops, or rogue DHCP servers that take down the whole network.
- If you need more ports, ask staff.

## 4. High-draw appliances

- Kettles, toasters, mini-fridges, heaters.
- The venue power is for PCs, not kitchen appliances.
