Tournaments add extra excitement to the LAN, but joining them is usually optional.

## Where to sign up

- Tournament sign-up is handled on the lobby portal: [lobby.ctrl-alt-gg.hu](https://lobby.ctrl-alt-gg.hu/)
- Some tournaments use a dedicated team page: [lobby.ctrl-alt-gg.hu/teams/](https://lobby.ctrl-alt-gg.hu/teams/)
- Deadlines are always announced for each event.

## Team size and lineup

- Team size depends on the game (for example, 1-3 players).
- If your roster is not full yet, it is still worth signing up.
- Some tournaments allow you to compete with fewer players.

## Important: finalize the team sign-up

- Creating a team and adding members is not enough.
- The team captain must also click the final tournament sign-up action.
- Without that final step, your team may not count as entered.

## Recruiting teammates

- If you are missing players, recruit on Discord or at the event.
- Many strong lineups are formed from mixed groups on-site.

## Ticket requirement

- Tournament participation requires a valid event ticket.
- In team tournaments, every listed member needs a valid ticket.

For ticket details, see [Tickets & Registration](/posts/tickets-and-registration/).
