If you want a smooth start, sort out your ticket and registration early.

## Where to handle it

- You can manage most things on the lobby portal: [lobby.ctrl-alt-gg.hu](https://lobby.ctrl-alt-gg.hu/)
- This is where you register, pay, and check your current status.
- Exact deadlines are always announced with the specific event.

## Pre-departure check

Before you leave:

- Confirm your ticket is **valid**.
- Check that all payments are completed.
- If anything looks wrong, report it early on Discord or to organizers.

## Entry at the venue

- At check-in, you must show your ticket barcode.
- Phone display is preferred, but a printed screenshot is also accepted.
- Save it offline too, so weak internet does not block your entry.

## Tickets and tournaments

- Tournament participation requires a valid ticket.
- For team tournaments, every team member must have a valid ticket.

For team flow details, see [Tournaments](/posts/tournaments/).
For your full pre-event prep, see [Before You Arrive](/posts/before-you-arrive/).
