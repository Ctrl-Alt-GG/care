If you plan your trip before the last minute, you save yourself a lot of stress.

## Before you leave

- Check your route the day before.
- Have a backup option in case of traffic, delays, or cancellations.
- Add buffer time, especially if you are carrying a full setup.

## Driving and carpooling

- If you bring a lot of gear, driving is usually the easiest option.
- Use community channels (Discord, carpool sheets) to find travel buddies.
- If you have free seats, help others get to the event.
- Park only in designated parking areas.

## Public transport

- Check live schedules before departure.
- Verify whether your pass/ticket is valid for the specific line.
- Timing matters even more on late or low-frequency routes.

## Arrival window

- Follow the announced arrival window for each event.
- Try not to arrive in the last few minutes.
- The check-in crew cannot stay at the entrance all day.

## After arrival

- Keep your ticket and barcode ready while queuing.
- Unpack quickly and neatly so everyone can settle in smoothly.
